About Mealpler

Hello and welcome to Mealpler. It is a simple and easy to use meal planning manager.
My name is Inna and I've initially developed Mealpler to optimize a routine food planning. I've struggled a lot with planning food for week. Unfortunately, random meal planners couldn't provide the experience I wanted. That's why I've decided to make an own one to work as I expect. Hope you'll enjoy this experience too.
My main goal was to reduce the amount of thins you need to calculate - grams, litres etc. That's because I just keep in mind PORTIONS. And so do most of people.
So do this app - it allows you to concentrate on the thing that really matters - portion.
